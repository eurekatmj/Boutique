Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days across India. Express delivery (2-3 days) is available for select pin codes. Free shipping on orders above ₹2999.

What is your return policy?

We offer easy 30-day returns on all items in original condition with tags attached. Simply initiate a return through your account or contact our support team.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking link via email and SMS. You can also track your order through the 'My Orders' section in your account.

Are the products authentic?

Absolutely. Every product at Maison Reine is 100% authentic and handpicked by our curation team. We work directly with certified artisans and established manufacturers.
