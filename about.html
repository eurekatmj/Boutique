Our Story

Our Beginning

Born from a Passion for Excellence

Maison Reine was founded with one belief: every woman deserves to feel like royalty. Starting from a small atelier in Mumbai, we have grown into one of India's most respected luxury fashion houses, serving thousands of discerning women across the country.

Our collections blend the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship with contemporary silhouettes, creating pieces that are timeless yet relevant — perfect for the modern woman who honors tradition while embracing today.
